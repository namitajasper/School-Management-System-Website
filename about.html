About Pheonix Rising Highscool

At Phoenix Rising High School, we believe in the power of a new beginning. Our school motto, Ab Cinere Resurgimus, translates from Latin to "Out of the Ashes, We Rise." This motto reflects our commitment to providing a supportive and empowering environment for students who seek a different path to academic success.
We understand that traditional learning environments may not always be the best fit for every student. Phoenix Rising High School offers alternative education programs designed to re-engage and motivate students who are looking to get back on track. In our safe and nurturing learning environment, we focus on individualized education plans that cater to each student's unique strengths and needs.
Our dedicated team of educators is passionate about helping students discover their potential and achieve their academic goals. We offer a variety of curriculum options and a personalized approach to learning that fosters a sense of accomplishment and self-confidence.
At Phoenix Rising High School, we're more than just a school; we're a community that celebrates second chances and supports students on their journey to a brighter future. Join us and rise from the ashes with us!

One School, Two Campus

Our One School, Two Campuses model ensures that our students, no matter their campus placement, enjoy the same high-quality educational experience in premium, purpose-built facilities under the strategic direction of the Board of Trustees of the Pheonix Rising Foundation and in line with the PRHS Vision, Mission, and Core Values. There is a synergy between our campuses that results from our shared sense of purpose and direction, the close collaboration between our leadership teams, faculty and staff members, and the strong sense of community among our families.
